Deep Listening

1989

This is the first recording by the Deep Listening Band, whose members are Pauline Oliveros, Stuart Dempster and Panaitois. It was recorded in the Fort Worden Cistern in Port Townsend, WA in 1988. The cistern has a 45-second reverb. This was the first in a series of recordings that the band made in other acousitically-extraoridnary sites.
