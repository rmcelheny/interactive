From 2012-2013 Travis, Josh and Dan ran American Medium as a pop-up gallery in Union Square.

They had five short-run shows at that space: Brenna Murphy; Andrew Norman Wilson; a group show with Kari Altmann, Benjamin Phelan, Borna Sammak and Travess Smalley; a night of performances by Jake Dibeler and Medallion Sinclair, and Ann Hirsch; and Jon Rafman...

The show with Jon Rafman was a recreation of works from a digital environment.
