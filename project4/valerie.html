To Valerie Solanas and Marilyn Monroe in Recognition of their Desperation

1970

From a 2012 interview with composer Anne Lebaron:

To Valerie Solanas and Marilyn Monroe in Recognition of Their Desperation (1970) is also an orchestra piece. I lost interest some time ago in standard configurations of instruments and really prefer mixed inclusive ensembles that include a diversity of instruments of the world. Players tend to loosen up when they are not a part of a standard grouping.
